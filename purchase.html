Purchase & Return Policy

Ordering Process

  • All orders require a minimum 50% deposit
  • Custom orders require full payment before production begins
  • Standard lead time is 2-3 weeks after payment confirmation
  • Rush orders available for additional fee (contact for details)

Shipping Policy

  • Free shipping on orders over $500
  • Standard shipping: 3-5 business days ($15 flat rate)
  • Express shipping: 1-2 business days ($30 flat rate)
  • International shipping available (rates vary by destination)

Return Policy

  • Custom manufactured items cannot be returned
  • Standard items may be returned within 14 days of receipt
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Customer pays return shipping costs
  • Refunds processed within 5 business days of receiving returned items
